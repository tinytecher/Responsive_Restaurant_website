ABOUT

Follow the flowers to find one of Koh Samui's best kept secrets! With delious food, amazing cocktails and live music, Treehouse Silent Garden is the prefect spot to chill out and enjoy the sunshine.

Food & Drink

Fresh cuisine created with local produce. Menu highlights include fresh vegetable rice paper rolls, Massaman curry that melts in the mouth and an extensive wine and cocktails list.

Relaxation

This super-chill spot is the place to relax. Boasting a beachfront location with incredible views and plenty of spots to relax with beanbags, sunbeds and hammocks aplenty.

Entertainment

Weekly events include outdoor cinema nights, Oysters & Wine nights, live music, fire shows and our chill Sundays with delicious Sunday Roast & drink specials.
